Good to know

Questions, answered

Your doctor reviews your health history, lifestyle and main concerns, then assesses your constitution (dosha) and pulse. You leave with a clear, personalised plan rather than a one-size-fits-all prescription.

Our doctors take your full history into account and will discuss how Ayurvedic care fits with any treatment you are already receiving. Always tell your doctor about current medications. Ayurveda supports wellbeing and is not a replacement for emergency or specialist medical care.

We offer insurance reimbursement support and accept insurance cards for claims, subject to terms and your provider's coverage. Our team will guide you through what applies before you start.

It depends on your concern, how long it has been present and the plan you follow. Some people feel relief quickly, others need a full programme. Results vary from person to person, and your doctor will set honest expectations.

We have clinics in Dubai (Silicon Oasis), Sharjah (New Muweileh) and Ajman (Al Liwara). Choose whichever is closest, or message us and we will point you to the right doctor for your concern.
